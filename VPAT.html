The Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) is uesed to evaluate accessibility standards for Section 508 (U.S.), EN 301 549 (EU), and W3C/WAI WCAG. Some companies are required to fill out a VPAT before they can relese a new produce, others may need one to Certify that they have meet the Accessibilty requirements. We use the VPAT to certify that our sites are ADA compliant.

VPAT Evaluation

A VPAT Evaluation is a process we use to examin your site and verify that accessibility standards have been stified to level AA. During this process we will crawl your site, use eveluation tools, as-well-as manualy go through the site using assitive technologies. We test the site for hands-free access, vision impairedness, low-vision, color-blind, learning disabilities, deafness or low-hearing, just to name a few

VPAT Certifications

Our certification process is easy. After we compleat an evauation we use one of the four (4) VPAT documents to Certifications your site meets the required standards and is compliance with Sections 508, EU, or WCAG.

  • VPAT 508 Certification: U.S. Federal accessibility standard
  • VPAT EU Certification: European Union’s Accessibility requirements
  • VPAT WCAG Certification: W3C/WAI’s Web Content Accessibility Guideline
  • VPAT Full Certification: Incorporates all three of the above standards

All VPAT® Certifications contain:

  • Product Description that describes the product
  • Evaluation Methods used to test the product’s accessibility
  • Notes to provide additional details concerning the product tested and the evaluation methods

We provide a PDF you can disply on your website for download or send to users upon request.
