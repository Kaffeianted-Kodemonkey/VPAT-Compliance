Overview

VPAT is the Voluntary Product Accessibility Template to evaluate accessibility standards for Section 508 (U.S.), EN 301 549 (EU), and W3C/WAI WCAG. Some companies are required to fill one out before they can relese a new produce, others may need one to Certify that they have meet the Accessibilty requirements.

Certifications

Kaffeinated Kodemonkey offers four (4) VPAT Certifications to verify compliance with Sections 508 & WCAG.

  • VPAT 508 Certification: U.S. Federal accessibility standard
  • VPAT EU Certification: European Union’s Accessibility requirements
  • VPAT WCAG Certification: W3C/WAI’s Web Content Accessibility Guideline
  • VPAT Full Certification: Incorporates all three of the above standards

All VPAT® Certifications contain:

  • Product Description that describes the product
  • Evaluation Methods used to test the product’s accessibility
  • Notes to provide additional details concerning the product tested and the evaluation methods

We provide a PDF you can disply on your website for download or send to users upon request.

Disclamer

Please Note: A VPAT® Certification can be an essential aid in assessing the accessibility of products, it is important to note that in cases where a product conforms to relevant standards and technical specifications, an end user may still encounter difficulties utilizing the site due to the nature or severity of his or her disability. On the other hand, a product that may not fully conform to all technical requirements may still be perfectly accessible to an end user who has a disability, but does not need a particular accessibility feature, e.g., a large-button telephone handset for an individual with a hearing disability.

While there may not be a perfectly accessible product, many can still be used effectively and efficiently by most people with disabilities. Kaffeinated Kodemonkey offers to assist companiend with understanding a VPAT.
